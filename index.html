About

Hello, my name is Tri Ahmad Irfan. I’m a first year Computer Science student at University of Indonesia. I love programming and designing things. Sometimes I make websites and write anything I like. I know several programming languages and always eager to learn a new one.

When I have leisure times, I’d love to grab a book and enjoy the moment out of it. I really appreciate a well-designed product. Someday, I want to be an IT-preneur, an inspiring writer, and a word class designer.

Currently, I run a small web design studio named Coder Hutan Studio.
